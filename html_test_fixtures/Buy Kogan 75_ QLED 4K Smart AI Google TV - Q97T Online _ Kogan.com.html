    Product Details

    Transform your home entertainment with the Kogan™ 75” QLED 4K Smart AI Google TV. Enjoy breathtaking colour and clarity, smart features and enhanced performance for movies, gaming and everything in between.

    • QLED technology. Quantum Dot technology delivers vibrant, true-to-life colours and superior brightness.
    • DLG 120Hz technology. Experience smoother visuals and more responsive gameplay on a 60Hz panel.
    • 4K Ultra HD resolution. See every detail with razor-sharp, immersive picture quality.
    • MEMC and ALLM. Motion Estimation, Motion Compensation and Auto Low Latency Mode reduce blur and stutter for smoother action, sports and gaming.
    • Dolby Vision™ & Atmos™. Create a cinematic immersion with lifelike and vibrant visuals and immersive sound.
    • High-Dynamic Range. Brighter highlights, improved contrast and richer, more realistic colours.
    • Google Play Store. Access 400,000+ movies, TV episodes and live TV with ease.
    • Built-in Google Chromecast. Stream content directly from your smartphone, laptop or tablet.
    • Multiple USB and HDMI inputs. Plug in and play your favourite media effortlessly.
    • Kogan TV+ now on Google Play! Stream heaps of free live channels – sports, movies, music, shows and more. Learn more: www.kogan.com/au/kogan-tv-plus

    Immersive visuals with QLED and HDR technology

    QLED technology enhances colour accuracy and depth, ensuring vibrant, true-to-life visuals from nearly every angle. Combined with HDR10 support, it delivers exceptional contrast and detail, bringing out vivid hues and stunning clarity in both bright and dark scenes.

    What is Google TV?

    Google TV is an innovative platform that transforms your TV into a personalised entertainment hub. It brings all your favourite streaming services together in one place, making it easier to discover, watch and control your content.

    Your Android phone can serve as a convenient remote control, simplifying navigation through searches, streaming apps and browsing, allowing you to effortlessly dive into your favourite shows and movies.

    Plus, Google TV lets you control your entire Google Smart Home from your TV. View feeds from video doorbells and security cameras, control smart lights, jump on a Google Duo call and more from your TV or by using the Google Assistant capability to simply say, “Okay, Google.”

    AI-personalised content, delivered right to your living room

    Tired of wasting time trying to find the perfect movie or show to watch? With Google TV’s new AI-based recommendations, curated suggestions are tailored to your personal preferences. Searching for new content has never been easier, thanks to AI-generated descriptions that eliminate the need to watch every trailer before you press play!

    Fluid gameplay with DLG support

    Enjoy smoother, more responsive gameplay with DLG (Dual Line Gate) support. DLG simulates 120Hz motion on a 60Hz panel and works with VRR up to 120Hz*, reducing input lag and motion blur. The result? Fast-paced action looks fluid and sharp – perfect for competitive gaming.

    *at up to 2K resolution

    Smoother motion with MEMC

    MEMC (Motion Estimation and Motion Compensation) enhances fast-moving scenes by reducing blur and stutter. Action, sports and games appear smoother, clearer and more lifelike for a more immersive experience.

    Level up your gaming thanks to ALLM

    Gain a competitive edge with ALLM, which automatically optimises display settings for gaming. By reducing input lag and boosting responsiveness, it ensures smooth, lag-free gameplay, so every action feels fast and precise when it counts.

    Cinematic QLED visuals with Dolby Vision™ and Dolby Atmos™

    QLED technology delivers vibrant colour, high brightness and sharp clarity, while Dolby Vision™ enhances contrast and detail scene by scene for a more realistic picture. Dolby Atmos™ adds immersive, multidimensional sound that surrounds the room, creating a true cinematic experience for movies, sport and streaming.

    Stunning clarity with Ultra HD

    With jaw-dropping 4K resolution and stunning high dynamic range, the Kogan 75” QLED 4K Smart AI Google TV delivers intense colour displays and impressive contrast.

    Full streaming access

    Effortlessly launch the pre-installed Netflix, YouTube and Amazon Prime Video, access the Google Play Store to download SBS On Demand, ABC iview and more of your favourite apps or stream from your smart device using the built-in Google Chromecast.
